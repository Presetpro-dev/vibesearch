# Service-Area Page Blueprint

The section order for every generated service-area page. Each section states its job. Skip a section only if the business genuinely has nothing for it, and never skip more than one.

## 1. H1 + opening answer (the citability block)
H1 names the service and the place naturally ("Wedding Photography in Kingston, Ontario"). The first paragraph is a complete, self-contained answer to "who does this, where, and why should I trust them": business name, the service, the area served, and the single strongest proof point. Two to four sentences. This paragraph is what AI search engines lift, so it must stand alone.

## 2. Local proof (the anti-doorway core)
The 3+ real elements collected before generation: recent jobs in the area with specifics, photos taken there, neighborhoods and landmarks actually served, area logistics (travel fees, response times, permits). Written as narrative, not a list of city names. This section is why the page deserves to exist.

## 3. The service, concretely
What is included, how the process works start to finish, honest pricing or a real range if the business will state one. Specific beats complete: three concrete details outrank ten vague ones.

## 4. Area-specific FAQ (3 to 5 questions)
Questions a customer in that place would actually ask ("Do you travel to X?", "How fast can you get to Y?"). Question-phrased H3s, answer-first sentences beneath. Real FAQPage markup is optional and only as a machine-readability signal; never promise rich results from it.

## 5. Reviews from that area
If any exist, quote one or two with first name and neighborhood. If none exist, omit the section entirely rather than faking it.

## 6. Call to action
One primary action matching the profile goal (call, book, quote form), the canonical NAP block, and hours.

## Per-page metadata checklist
- Title: `{Service} in {Place} | {Business}` or a natural variant, under ~60 characters
- Meta description: the offer plus the strongest local proof point, under ~155 characters
- One H1 only
- LocalBusiness/Service JSON-LD scoped with areaServed for this location
- 2 internal links: one to the main service page, one to an adjacent area or relevant post
- Unique photos per page whenever they exist; never reuse one hero across all locations
